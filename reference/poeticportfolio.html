The Artist Behind the Canvas

Hi, I'm Banu, a passionate painter who believes that every stroke of the brush carries a piece of the soul. My journey began in childhood, sketching dreams on napkins, and has evolved into a lifelong love affair with color and form.

Through my work, I explore the intersection of emotion and nature, finding inspiration in everything from morning sunlight filtering through leaves to the quiet contemplation of rainy afternoons.
